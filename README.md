# Lunar Balance & Safe Energy Initiative (LBSEI)

## Overview
A global safety framework to prevent high-risk industrialization on the Moon while enabling safe space energy innovation.

## Why This Matters
The Moon plays a critical role in stabilizing Earth's system.  
Unregulated industrial activity introduces systemic risks.

## What We Propose
- No industrial factories on the Moon
- No chain-reaction energy systems
- Mandatory AI-based simulation before deployment

## Innovation Path
- RabbitRams distributed energy systems
- Safe Fluidized Energy Field
- AI-controlled safety validation
